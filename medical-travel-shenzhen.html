Support for international patients planning non-emergency, short-stay healthcare visits in Shenzhen.

Medical Travelers

Visitors who want to combine a planned Shenzhen trip with health checkups, dental consultation, digestive screening, or fertility assessment coordination.

Foreign Residents and Business Travelers

People already in China who need help with hospital communication, translation, scheduling, and navigation.

Patients Comparing China Options

How It Works

A practical workflow from first inquiry to post-visit follow-up.

Send Your Request

Share the service you are considering, your preferred dates, language needs, and any relevant documents.

Coordination Review

We review whether the request and timeline appear practical for a planned Shenzhen visit.

Appointment Support

We help coordinate appointment options and confirm required documents and practical steps.

Travel Preparation

We help align arrival timing, hotel location, local transportation, and visit schedule.

In-Person Support

When arranged, we support registration, translation, navigation, and practical communication during the visit.

Follow-Up

We help organize reports, translation needs, and post-visit communication with the healthcare institution.

IntersectionMed is not a hospital or healthcare provider. We do not provide diagnosis, treatment, or medical advice. Medical decisions are made solely by licensed physicians and healthcare institutions.

What We Do Not Do

Clear boundaries help patients make safe, realistic plans.

No Emergency or Critical Care

IntersectionMed supports planned visits. For urgent symptoms or emergency needs, contact local emergency services or go directly to an appropriate medical facility.

No Diagnosis or Treatment Advice

We can coordinate a consultation, but we cannot decide what care is medically appropriate for you.

No Outcome or Cost Guarantees

Appointment availability, medical decisions, pricing, and outcomes depend on healthcare institutions and individual circumstances.

Common Questions

Practical answers for international patients planning a Shenzhen visit.

How long should I stay in Shenzhen?

Many Phase 1 services are planned around 1-7 days, but timing depends on the service, hospital availability, preparation requirements, and physician assessment.

Can you choose a hospital for me?

We can help coordinate appointment options based on service type, location, language support, and availability. We do not make "best hospital" claims or medical judgments.

Can you translate during the appointment?

Yes, English-Chinese translation and in-person accompaniment can be arranged depending on schedule and service scope.

Do you provide medical advice?

No. IntersectionMed does not provide diagnosis, treatment, prescriptions, or medical advice. Medical decisions are made by licensed physicians and healthcare institutions.
