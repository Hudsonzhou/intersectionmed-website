About IntersectionMed

Bridging the gap between international patients and China's quality healthcare system

Our Story

IntersectionMed was founded with a simple mission: to make China's exceptional healthcare accessible to patients worldwide. We understand the frustration of long waiting lists, exorbitant costs, and limited options in many Western healthcare systems.

Our team brings together medical professionals, international coordinators, and language specialists who have witnessed firsthand how China's efficient, affordable, and advanced medical care can transform lives.

Based in Shenzhen—a premier global destination for medical innovation—we are strategically positioned to connect international patients with quality healthcare facilities across the Greater Bay Area.
