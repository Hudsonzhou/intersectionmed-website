Colonoscopy and Endoscopy in Shenzhen: Preparation and Translation Support

Digestive screening visits require careful timing, clear preparation instructions, and accurate communication. This guide explains how international patients can plan ahead.

Planning the Timeline

Digestive screening may require preparation before the procedure and follow-up afterward.

Allow Enough Time

Many patients should allow 2-3 days, depending on consultation, preparation, procedure availability, and physician advice.

Translate Instructions

Preparation instructions must come from the provider. IntersectionMed can help translate and coordinate them.

Plan Rest Time

Some patients may need rest or companion support after a procedure, depending on provider guidance.

Documents to Share

Relevant medical context helps the provider understand your case.

  • Previous endoscopy or colonoscopy reports
  • Digestive symptoms or concerns to discuss with the physician
  • Medication list, especially blood thinners or chronic medications
  • Allergies and prior procedure history
  • Preferred dates and companion needs
