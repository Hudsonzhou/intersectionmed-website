Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get surgery scheduled?

In most cases, we can arrange consultations within 48 hours and surgeries within 1-2 weeks of your arrival in China. Emergency cases can be expedited further.

Do doctors speak English?

Yes. We partner with hospitals that have international departments staffed with English-speaking doctors. We also provide professional medical interpreters for all consultations and procedures.

What about medical visas?

We provide official invitation letters from partnering hospitals to support your medical visa application. Our team guides you through the entire visa process.

Is it safe to travel to China for medical care?

Absolutely. China's top-tier hospitals meet international standards (JCI accredited) and have been serving international patients for decades. We only partner with hospitals that have proven track records with foreign patients.
