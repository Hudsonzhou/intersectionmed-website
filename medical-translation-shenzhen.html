What to Prepare

Bringing the right information helps hospitals and clinics understand your request more efficiently.

  • Appointment details if already booked
  • Hospital or clinic address
  • Medical documents needing translation support
  • Preferred communication language and companion needs
IntersectionMed is not a hospital or healthcare provider. We do not provide diagnosis, treatment, or medical advice. Medical decisions are made solely by licensed physicians and healthcare institutions.

Common Questions

Clear answers for international patients planning ahead.

Is this certified translation?

General medical communication support is different from certified translation. If certified translation is needed, a separate certified provider may be required.

Can you interpret clinical advice?

We translate and facilitate communication. Clinical advice and decisions must come from licensed physicians.

Can you help after the appointment?

Yes. We can help organize reports, translate follow-up communication, and support practical next steps.

Send us your preferred dates, service type, language needs, and any relevant documents. We will help you understand the practical next steps.
