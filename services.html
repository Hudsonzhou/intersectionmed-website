How Service Coordination Works

A simple process from first inquiry to follow-up support.

Inquiry

Tell us your requested service, dates, passport country, language needs, and relevant medical documents.

Review

We check whether the request and timeline appear practical for a planned Shenzhen visit.

Coordination

We help coordinate appointment options and clarify documents, preparation, and logistics.

Travel Planning

We help align hotel location, transport, arrival timing, and the appointment schedule.

On-Site Support

We assist with registration, translation, navigation, and practical support during the visit.

Follow-Up

We help organize reports, translation needs, and post-visit communication.

IntersectionMed does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or medical advice. Clinical decisions are made by licensed physicians and healthcare institutions.
