Frequently Asked Questions

What languages do your interpreters speak?

We currently offer English-language services. Other languages may be arranged upon request—please contact us to discuss your specific needs.

How quickly can you arrange a hospital appointment?

For non-emergency cases, we can typically arrange appointments within 24-48 hours. For specialist consultations, scheduling 3-5 days in advance ensures the best doctor availability.

What happens if I need emergency care?

Contact us directly and we'll assist you as soon as possible. We'll coordinate with the nearest emergency room and ensure you receive prompt care while handling all communications.

Are your services covered by insurance?

Our medical escort services are typically out-of-pocket expenses. However, we can assist you in understanding your insurance coverage and can provide detailed invoices for reimbursement claims where applicable.

How much do your services cost?

Service packages start from ¥2,000 RMB per day depending on your needs. We provide transparent pricing after your free initial consultation. No hidden fees—you'll know exactly what's included.
