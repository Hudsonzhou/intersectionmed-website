Can Foreigners Use Hospitals in Shenzhen?

Foreign patients can access many healthcare institutions in Shenzhen, but appointment process, language support, documents, and payment steps vary by hospital and department.

Yes, foreign patients can access many hospitals and clinics in Shenzhen. The practical experience depends on the hospital, department, service type, appointment availability, language support, and required documents.

What Foreign Patients Usually Need

  • Passport information
  • Contact details
  • Reason for visit in simple terms
  • Relevant previous reports or images if available
  • Preferred appointment dates
  • Language support needs

When Appointment Coordination Helps

Coordination is useful when you are unsure which department to contact, need English-Chinese communication support, are planning a short stay, or want help with hospital registration and navigation.

Language Support

English support varies across institutions and departments. IntersectionMed can support appointment communication, interpretation during visits, and follow-up document communication when arranged in advance.

Common Planned Visit Types

Current Phase 1 support focuses on planned, non-emergency services such as health checkups, digestive screening, dental consultation, fertility assessment, dermatology, and medical translation.

What IntersectionMed Does Not Do

IntersectionMed does not claim access to the best hospital, does not provide medical triage, and does not replace consultation with licensed healthcare professionals. For urgent symptoms, patients should contact emergency services or go directly to an appropriate medical facility.
