Our role is coordination, translation, and practical support before, during, and after your Shenzhen appointment.

Step 1

Step 2

Step 4

What to Prepare

Bringing the right information helps hospitals and clinics understand your request more efficiently.

  • Passport information and contact details
  • Previous health reports, if available
  • Medication list and allergies, if relevant
  • Preferred dates and expected Shenzhen stay length
IntersectionMed is not a hospital or healthcare provider. We do not provide diagnosis, treatment, or medical advice. Medical decisions are made solely by licensed physicians and healthcare institutions.

Common Questions

Clear answers for international patients planning ahead.

How long does a health checkup take?

Many health checkups can be completed within 1-2 days, depending on package scope, appointment availability, and whether additional tests are requested by physicians.

Do I need to speak Chinese?

No. IntersectionMed can support English-Chinese communication during coordination and the hospital visit.

Can you explain my results?

We can assist with translation and communication. Medical interpretation of results and clinical recommendations must come from licensed physicians.
